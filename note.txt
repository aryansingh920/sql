1. The Music Catalog (The "What")
This is where the actual content lives.

artist: Names of the performers (e.g., AC/DC).

album: Titles of the collections (e.g., Let There Be Rock).

genre: Music categories (e.g., Rock, Jazz).

media_type: The format of the file (e.g., MPEG audio, AAC).

2. The Business Side (The "Who" & "How Much")
This tracks the money and the people.

customer: Personal info for everyone who bought music.

employee: The staff at the store (who reports to whom).

invoice: The "Header" of a sale (Date, Total, Billing Address).

invoice_line: The "Line Items" (Which specific track was bought in that invoice).

3. The Organization (The "Playlists")
playlist: The names of user-created lists.

playlist_track: The "Bridge" table that connects tracks to playlists.
